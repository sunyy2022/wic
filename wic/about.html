Who We Are

We’re Daisy and Marco – two curious souls bonded by our love for travel, cross-cultural storytelling, and steaming baskets of xiaolongbao. Having called Paris, San Francisco, and Melbourne home at different stages of our lives, we’ve learned one universal truth: the best travel memories are made where locals breathe, laugh, and live. Now back in China, we’re on a mission to help you experience our homeland through the lens of those who know its heartbeat best – its people.

Why Travel With Us?

🧭 Cultural Translators: Having navigated life abroad, we understand the unspoken questions travelers carry.

🏙 City Whisperers: We decode urban landscapes – from Shanghai’s futuristic skyline to Xi’an’s ancient alleyways.

🍜 Food Anthropologists: Our rule? Never recommend a restaurant we haven’t personally tested three times.

🗣 Your Safety Net: From bargaining phrases to subway hacks, we prepare you for the China guidebooks don’t mention.
